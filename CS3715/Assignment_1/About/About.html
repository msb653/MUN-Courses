As Newfoundland and Labrador's only university, We are devoted to excellence in teaching, learning and research and to develop leaders in many disciplines who make a difference globally. Federal University of Newfoundland(FUN) is a place without limits and we aren't afraid to ask bigger questions, to get better answers.

Established in 1885, the Federal University of Newfoundland(FUN) with two campuses, averages an enrolment of approximately 14,400 undergraduate and 3,500 graduate students every year. FUN has more than 85,000 alumni around the world.

In Newfoundland and Labrador we offer nearly 100 full-time degree programs and more than 300 part-time courses.
