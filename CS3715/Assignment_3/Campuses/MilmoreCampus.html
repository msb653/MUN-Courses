Milmore Campus

Milmore Campus is a small, interdisciplinary campus located in picturesque Milmore, Newfoundland, Canada, a city of 20,000 with a population of 45,000 in the surrounding area. Milmore has all the amenities of a larger city with the charm of a smaller town. Milmore is located on the island portion of Newfoundland and Labrador, the eastern-most province of Canada. The population of approximately half a million people is spread out over a large geographical area. Our province is known for friendly, welcoming people and a clean, healthy and safe environment.
